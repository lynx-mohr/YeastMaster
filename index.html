The Soul of beer.

We believe the true soul of beer lives within the yeast. As the old brewing adage goes: "The brewer makes wort, but the yeast makes beer." There is a profound wonder in how these microscopic artisans transform a simple, sweet liquid into the infinitely complex and soulful elixir we call beer.

Soulof.beer is a tribute to this biological magic. Whether you are here to explore our library of historic strains or to take full command of your fermentation, this is your laboratory. Monitor your brew in real-time, sync custom profiles to your YeastMaster-unit, and master the art of fermentation.

YEASTMASTER

Take absolute command of your fermentation. YeastMaster is a dedicated hardware unit and cloud ecosystem designed for the modern homebrewer, bridging the gap between the biological art of brewing and precise data control.

📟 The Hardware Unit

A dedicated physical controller with a crisp OLED display. Click to explore how it controls your fridge and heating element.

→

☁️ The Cloud App

FREE

Monitor your fermentation from anywhere in the world. Build custom temperature profiles in the Profiler, manage your dry hop schedules, and sync everything effortlessly to your physical unit. Completely free to use with standard 14-minute data syncs (high-frequency Pro-tier coming soon!).

🔬 The Yeast Library

FREE

Stop guessing. Our ever-growing, free-to-access library contains detailed guides on how to treat the world's most popular yeast strains to coax out the perfect esters and phenols for your specific beer style.

THE HARDWARE

YeastMaster is built around a custom ESP32 brain, designed to sit seamlessly on your fermentation chamber. It operates 100% locally, meaning your beer is safe even if the WiFi drops.

📺 Retro OLED Interface

A high-contrast OLED screen paired with a tactile rotary encoder gives you a classic, laboratory-grade interface. Adjust offsets, change yeast profiles, or trigger a cold crash right at the fridge.

[ Image of the OLED Screen ]

🌡️ Dual Temp Probes

Precision requires data. YeastMaster uses two detachable, waterproof sensors. One measures the ambient air in the fridge, while the other sits directly in the beer (or taped to the fermenter), allowing the PID logic to prevent massive temperature swings.

[ Image of the Temperature Probes ]

⚡ Wireless Relays

No dangerous high-voltage wiring. YeastMaster sends 433MHz radio signals directly to wireless smart plugs. One for your fridge (cooling), and one for a heat mat (heating). Total control, zero electrical hazards.
